Our History

Since 2016, BT Remodeling has been transforming spaces and creating value in Texas and beyond.

We handle projects end-to-end, from planning and material selection to finishing details. Our commercial services cover metal framing, drywall, doors, and acoustical ceilings. For residential work, we specialize in kitchens, bathrooms, flooring, paint, and exterior upgrades.
